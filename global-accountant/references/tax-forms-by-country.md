## India {#india}

### ITR Form Selector
| Form | Who Files |
|---|---|
| ITR-1 (Sahaj) | Resident individual; salary/pension + one house property + other sources; income ≤ ₹50L; no capital gains |
| ITR-2 | Individuals/HUF with capital gains, multiple properties, foreign income/assets, NRI |
| ITR-3 | Individuals/HUF with income from business/profession |
| ITR-4 (Sugam) | Individuals/HUF/Firms under presumptive taxation (44AD, 44ADA, 44AE) |
| ITR-5 | Firms, LLPs, AOPs, BOIs |
| ITR-6 | Companies (other than claiming 11/12 exemption) |
| ITR-7 | Trusts, political parties, institutions claiming exemptions |

### Key Dates (Individuals, FY2024-25)
| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Advance Tax Q1 | 15 June 2025 |
| Advance Tax Q2 | 15 September 2025 |
| Advance Tax Q3 | 15 December 2025 |
| Advance Tax Q4 | 15 March 2026 |
| ITR Filing (non-audit) | 31 July 2025 |
| ITR Filing (audit cases) | 31 October 2025 |
| Belated / Revised Return | 31 December 2025 |

### Documents Needed
- Form 16 (salary TDS certificate)
- Form 26AS / AIS / TIS (tax credit statement)
- Bank statements
- Capital gains statements (CAMS, KFintech, broker)
- Rent receipts / tenancy agreement
- Investment proofs (80C, 80D, etc.)

## UAE {#uae}

### Forms & Requirements
| Form | When |
|---|---|
| Corporate Tax Return | Companies with taxable income (FTA portal) |
| VAT Return | VAT-registered businesses (quarterly or monthly) |
| Excise Tax Return | Producers/importers of excise goods |
| Transfer Pricing disclosure | If related-party transactions exceed AED 40M |

### Key Dates
| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Corporate Tax Return | Within 9 months of financial year end |
| VAT Return (quarterly) | 28th of month following quarter end |
| VAT registration threshold | AED 375,000 (mandatory); AED 187,500 (voluntary) |
