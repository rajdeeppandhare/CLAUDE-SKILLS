## India {#india}

### Individuals — Old Tax Regime
| Section | Deduction | Limit (₹) |
|---|---|---|
| 80C | PF, PPF, ELSS, LIC, NSC, home loan principal, tuition fees | 1,50,000 |
| 80CCD(1B) | NPS additional contribution | 50,000 |
| 80D | Health insurance premium (self+family / parents) | 25,000 / 50,000 |
| 80E | Education loan interest | No limit (8 years) |
| 80EEA | Home loan interest (affordable housing) | 1,50,000 |
| 80G | Charitable donations | 50–100% of donation |
| 80TTA | Savings account interest | 10,000 |
| 80TTB | Senior citizen bank interest | 50,000 |
| 24(b) | Home loan interest (self-occupied) | 2,00,000 |
| Standard Deduction | Salaried employees | 50,000 |
| HRA | House Rent Allowance (metro/non-metro formula) | Actual / formula |

### New Tax Regime (FY2024-25 onwards)
- Standard deduction: ₹75,000 (enhanced from ₹50,000)
- NPS employer contribution: deductible
- Most 80C/80D deductions NOT available
- Lower slab rates — better if deductions < ~₹3.75L

### Businesses
- Depreciation: WDV method under Income Tax Act; SLM under Companies Act
- 80IC: Profits from units in Special Category States
- 80IB: Profits from industrial undertakings
- 80JJAA: New employment deduction (30% of additional wages for 3 years)
- Startup deduction: 100% profit deduction for 3 out of 10 years (Sec 80-IAC)

## UAE {#uae}

- **No personal income tax** on individuals
- **Corporate Tax** (effective June 2023): 0% up to AED 375,000; 9% above
- Small Business Relief: available for revenue ≤ AED 3M
- Qualifying Free Zone Persons: 0% on qualifying income
- No capital gains tax (except real estate in some emirates)
- No withholding tax on dividends/interest/royalties
- VAT deduction: Input VAT recoverable for business purposes

## Singapore {#singapore}

### Individuals
| Relief | Limit (SGD) |
|---|---|
| Earned Income Relief | Up to $8,000 |
| CPF (Employee) | Deductible |
| NSman Relief | $3,000–$5,000 |
| Parent/Handicapped Parent Relief | $5,500–$14,000 |
| Course Fees Relief | $5,500 |
| SRS contributions | $15,300 (citizens/PR); $35,700 (foreigners) |
| Life Insurance Relief | Up to $5,000 |

### Businesses
- Corporate tax: 17% (flat)
- Partial tax exemption: first $10K at 75%; next $190K at 50%
- Startup exemption: first 3 years — first $100K at 75%; next $100K at 50%
- R&D: 150% deduction on qualifying expenditure
- Capital allowances: accelerated on plant & machinery

## Germany {#germany}

### Individuals
- Basic allowance (Grundfreibetrag): €11,604 (2024)
- Employee lump sum: €1,230
- Pension contributions: up to €27,566 (2024)
- Health/long-term care insurance premiums: deductible
- Church tax: 8–9% of income tax (deductible)
- Child allowance (Kinderfreibetrag): €6,384 per child
- Home office: €6/day up to €1,260/year

6. **R&D and innovation incentives**

Always verify with the local tax authority website or a licensed local tax professional.
