# Outreach Playbook

Trigger-event sequences, personalisation research patterns, LinkedIn variants, and industry-specific templates.

---

## Trigger Events

Trigger events are signals that a prospect may need your solution *right now*. Emails sent within 48–72 hours of a trigger event see 2–3x higher reply rates.

### High-Priority Triggers

| Trigger | Signal | Where to Find It |
|---|---|---|
| New hire in relevant role | They're building the function | LinkedIn, company announcements |
| Funding announcement | Budget unlocked, growing fast | Crunchbase, TechCrunch, LinkedIn |
| Job posting for role you help with | Pain point confirmed, budget exists | LinkedIn Jobs, Greenhouse, Lever |
| Company growth (headcount spike) | Scaling pains incoming | LinkedIn company page |
| Competitor departure or shutdown | Their current tool is disrupted | Twitter/X, Reddit, news |
| Executive change (new CRO, CMO, etc.) | New priorities, new budget | LinkedIn |
| Product launch / major announcement | They're in growth mode | ProductHunt, press releases |

### Trigger-Event Email Template

```
Subject: [Company] + [trigger event reference]

Hi [Name],

Saw [Company] just [trigger event — be specific]. Congrats on [outcome of trigger].

At this stage, a lot of [their role]s we work with are [specific challenge that comes with this growth].

We help [ICP] [outcome], usually within [timeframe]. [One customer example or stat].

Worth a quick chat to see if we could help with [specific thing]?

[Name]
```

## Personalisation Research Patterns

### For Tier 1 (1:1 high-value accounts)

In order of highest signal:

1. **Their recent content** — LinkedIn posts, articles, podcast appearances, conference talks
   - "Listened to your talk on [X] at [conference] — your point about [Y] resonated."
2. **Company news** — press releases, blog posts, product announcements
   - "Noticed [Company] just launched [X]. Timing felt relevant..."
3. **Their job posting language** — reveals pain and priority
   - "Saw you're hiring a [role] — sounds like [related challenge] is a priority right now."
4. **Their product/service** — shows you've done homework
   - "We use [Company]'s [product] for [thing] — noticed [specific observation]."

### For Tier 2 (role + industry personalisation)

Use ICP-level insights that feel personalised even at scale:

- "Most [their title]s we talk to are dealing with [specific pain] since [industry shift]."
- "Since [GPT / iOS 17 / whatever change], a lot of [role]s in [industry] have been [doing thing differently]."
- "At companies your size ([revenue or headcount range]), [specific challenge] usually surfaces around [stage]."

## LinkedIn Outreach Variants

LinkedIn messages should be shorter than email. 50–75 words max for connection requests.

### Connection Request (with note)
```
Hi [Name], [one specific observation about their work or company]. I work with [ICP] on [outcome] — thought there might be some overlap. Would love to connect.
```

### First Message After Connecting
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]. 

[Brief, relevant context — what you do, for who, one result].

[Low-friction question or CTA].
```

### LinkedIn + Email Combo
Reference one in the other:

Email: "Also sent you a LinkedIn connection request in case easier to connect there."
LinkedIn: "Sent you an email earlier this week re: [topic] — figured I'd say hi here too."

## Industry-Specific Templates

### SaaS → SaaS Outreach
```
Subject: [Company]'s [metric] / activation rate

Hi [Name],

Saw [Company] is scaling fast — [funding/headcount/launch reference].

Most growth-stage SaaS teams we talk to hit a wall when [specific pain: trial-to-paid conversion, activation rate, churn signals, etc.].

We helped [Customer] go from [metric] to [metric] in [timeframe]. 

Worth 15 minutes to see if we could do the same for [Company]?

[Name]
```

### Agency → Brand Outreach
```
Subject: [Brand] + [channel/outcome]

Hi [Name],

[Specific observation about their brand, recent campaign, or product].

We work with [ICP] brands on [specific outcome — not "marketing"]. Recent example: [Customer] [result].

Is [specific goal] something you're focused on this [quarter/year]?

[Name]
```

### Consultant / Solo Seller
```
Subject: quick question about [their thing]

Hi [Name],

[Personal observation — podcast, article, post, talk].

I help [ICP] [outcome] — usually [timeframe] to see results.

You'd know better than me, but [related challenge] seems like it comes up a lot for [their audience]. Happy to share what's worked if useful.

[Name]
```

## Follow-Up Frameworks

### The Value-Add Follow-Up
Don't just "check in." Bring something:
- A relevant article ("Thought this was relevant to what we discussed...")
- A data point or benchmark they might not have
- A short loom video showing your product solving their specific problem
- A case study from a company similar to theirs

### The Bump
When in doubt, a simple bump works:
```
Subject: Re: [original subject]

[Name] — bumping this up in case it got buried.

[One sentence restatement of value] — still happy to share more if the timing is right.

[Name]
```

### The Breakup Email
Closes the loop, often prompts replies from fence-sitters:
```
Subject: closing the loop

Hi [Name],

I've reached out a couple of times — I don't want to keep cluttering your inbox.

If [specific pain] ever becomes a priority, I'm happy to share how we've helped [similar company]. Just reply and I'll send over a quick summary.

Otherwise, best of luck with [their thing].

[Name]
```

## Deliverability Checklist

Before launching a sequence:

- [ ] Domain warmed up (new domains need 4–8 weeks of gradual sending)
- [ ] SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured
- [ ] Sending from a subdomain (protect your main domain)
- [ ] No more than 50–100 emails/day per inbox (scale gradually)
- [ ] Plain text or minimal HTML (no heavy design in cold email)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link included (legal requirement in most jurisdictions)
- [ ] List cleaned (remove invalid emails before sending)
- [ ] Test in spam checkers (mail-tester.com, GlockApps)
