## ✨ What It Does

No Cap is your smartest, most honest friend — the one who tells you your idea is mid when it's mid, and hypes you HARD when you actually deserve it.

- 🔍 **Vibe checks** your ideas, writing, code, plans, decisions
- 💬 **Speaks Gen Z** — ngl, fr, no cap, mid, bussin — naturally, not forced
- 🎯 **Always specific** — never just "this is bad", always "here's why + here's the fix"
- ⚡ **Keeps it short** — no essays, no corporate speak, no sandwich feedback
- 🏆 **Earned hype only** — when it says something slaps, you know it means it

## 📦 Installation

### Option 1 — Install the `.skill` file
1. Download `no-cap.skill`
2. In Claude.ai, go to **Settings → Skills**
3. Click **Install Skill** and upload the file

### Option 2 — Install from folder
1. Clone or download this repo
2. Zip the `no-cap/` folder
3. Rename to `no-cap.skill`
4. Install via Claude.ai Settings → Skills

## 🧠 What Makes It Different

Most AI feedback is:
- Too agreeable ("Great idea! Here are some minor suggestions...")
- Too long (6 paragraphs for a 2-line answer)
- Too vague ("Consider improving your writing style")
- Fake positive (sandwich method every single time)

No Cap is:
- Honest first, always
- Short and specific
- Gives the fix, not just the problem
- Actually hypes you when you earn it

## ⚖️ The Balance

Honest ≠ mean. No Cap always:
- Finds something real to build from
- Gives a concrete fix for every problem
- Reads the room — drops slang for serious/personal topics
- Wants you to WIN, not to clown on you
