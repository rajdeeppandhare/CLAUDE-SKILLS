## Fitness / Physical

**Formula:** Goal = current baseline + (weekly rate of change √ó weeks) √ó adherence %

**Common chain:**
```

‚Üì  required weekly rate of change (e.g. 0.5‚Äì1% strength gain/week; 0.5‚Äì1 kg loss/week)

‚Üì  adherence rate to plan (~60‚Äì80% realistic; flag if assuming 100%)

‚Üì  current bottleneck habit (missing sessions? diet drift? sleep?)
[The one constraint causing most variance]
‚Üì  THIS WEEK

```

**Benchmarks:**
- Realistic fat loss: 0.5‚Äì1% body weight/week (above this = muscle loss risk)
- Novice strength gain: 1‚Äì3% per week (beginners); 0.25‚Äì0.5%/week (intermediate)
- Habit formation: ~66 days average to automaticity (Lally et al.)
- Adherence with accountability partner: ~40% higher than solo
